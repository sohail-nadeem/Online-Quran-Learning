Tajweed Quran Online Classes

At Teach Me Quran, we offer professional Tajweed Quran online classes for kids and adults who wish to recite the Holy Quran with accuracy and beauty. Tajweed is the science of correct Quran pronunciation, ensuring every letter is recited from its proper articulation point.

Our qualified male and female teachers provide one-on-one interactive sessions, helping students master the rules of Tajweed step by step. Whether you are a beginner learning the basics or an advanced learner polishing your recitation, our Quran with Tajweed classes are tailored to your level. With flexible timings, engaging methods, and worldwide accessibility, Teach Me Quran makes learning Tajweed easy and effective. Build confidence in your recitation and experience the true beauty of the Quran.

Makharij (Articulation Points)

  • Correct pronunciation of Arabic letters
  • Differences between similar sounds
  • Practice with individual letters & words

Sifaat (Characteristics of Letters)

  • Heavy & light letters (Takhkeem & Tarqeeq)
  • Qalqalah letters
  • Madd (elongation) rules

Rules of Noon Sakin & Tanween

  • Izhar (clear pronunciation)
  • Idgham (merging)
  • Iqlab (changing)
